# Design System

Token-driven.

Derived from AI-selected:

- Brand positioning
- Tone
- Market perception

---

## Colors

System must include:

- Background
- Surface
- Primary
- Accent
- Text
- Muted
- Border

Premium rule:
Use neutral base + single accent

---

## Typography

Heading must reflect positioning:

| Positioning | Font |
|------------|------|
| Premium | Serif headline |
| Tech | Geometric sans |
| Lifestyle | Humanist |

Body:
Always readable sans-serif

---

## Spacing

Use 4px base scale.

---

## Radius

| Tone | Radius |
|------|--------|
| Luxury | Medium |
| Tech | Small |
| Friendly | Large |

---

## Shadow

Use soft depth.

Avoid harsh shadows.
